Big Data for Development Research Network

BD4D is a Southern-led partnership with the objective of developing policy relevant research on big data for development that is conceptualized and implemented by Southern organizations. It is supported by a grant from the International Development Research Centre (IDRC), Canada.

Network Partners

African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS)
Centro de Pensamiento Estratégico Internacional (CEPEI), Colombia
LIRNEasia, Sri Lanka
Local Development Research Institute (LDRI), Rwanda and Kenya
The Centre for Internet and Society (CIS), India
